Random Population Generator

1. Choose Tools from the main menu.

2. Choose Random… from the Tools menu.

3. Choose the desired distribution in the pop-up menu in the Random Distribution Panel.



In the Isotropic distribution, the universe is seeded with a constant density of lifeforms.  

Type a density in the density  cell and click Apply (or press the return key).


   

     

 

In the Gaussian distribution, the universe is seeded with a bell shaped distribution. The probability of finding a lifeform at a distance r  from the center is proportional to exp(-a r^2).  

Choose a width (as a percent of the total width) in the width cell, choose a density in the density  cell and click Apply. (or press the return key).

                 

             

 

In the Step distribution, the universe is seeded in the center only. All the population is inside a circle with a given radius. 

Choose a diameter (as a percent of the total width) in the width cell, choose a density in the density  cell and click Apply. (or press the return key).
